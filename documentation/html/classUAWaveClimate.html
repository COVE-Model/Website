Detailed Description

Four-bin PDF wave climate object following Ashton & Murray (2006).

Wave directions represented in a four-bin PDF of wave directions. Wave climate thus described by two parameters: asymmetry (A) and highness (U). Assymetry is the proportion of waves approaching from the upcoast direction (relative to the overall trend of the coastline). Highness is the proportion of waves that impinge the coastline at high angle (>45^o). See Ashton, A.D., Murray, A.B., 2006. High-angle wave instability and emergent shoreline shapes: 1. Modeling of sand waves, flying spits, and capes. J. Geophys. Res., 111(F4), F04011, doi:10.1029/2005jf000422.. Wave height and period represented by Gaussian distributions.
